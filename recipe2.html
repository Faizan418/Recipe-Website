Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • Salt and peper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pan, cook diced chicken until browned.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in curry powder, cumin, and coriander, and cook for in minute.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and coconut milk
  5. Simmer for 20-25 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with rice or naan bread.
