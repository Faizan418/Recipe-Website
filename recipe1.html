Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to tast

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separatepan, cook minced beef until browned.
  3. Add shopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in canned tomaties, tomato paste, and oregano.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
